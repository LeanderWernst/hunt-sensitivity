Sensitivity Calculator

Horizontal Screen Resolution

The amount of horizontal pixels of your screen resolution.

For Example 2560 for a screen resolution of 2560×1440

Vertical Screen Resolution

The amount of vertical pixels of your screen resolution.

For example 1440 for a screen resolution of 2560×1440

Field of View

The FOV value set in your game settings. Must be a value between 85-110.

Baseline Sensitivity

The sensivity setting used as baseline to calculate all other sensitivities.

You should probably use Shoulder Aim or Aim Down Sight.

Sensitivity

The sensitivity you want to convert to all aiming styles.

Monitor Distant Coefficient

A point (value between 0-1) on the monitor between the crosshair (0) and the edge of the monitor (1).
For this distance, the mouse will also travel the same distance.
A settings of 0 is recommended, as it equals the rate of change (speed) across all scopes.

Hipfire FOV

Whether you use normal or zoom in your ingame settings.
